Who is Artan?
~a designer and developer based out of Skopje, North Macedonia.
In the years of 2013/14, Artan, who was 11 years old at the time, stumbled upon a dusty computer, and from that moment, his world transformed. He turned it on, saw the monitor with a grasp, heard the hardware working... and watched the magic happen.


    The young boy's curiosity started to expand more every day. Computers were his everyday. He proceeded to take on this path for the rest of his life because he thought: "there can not be enough understanding of machines" He since then started to chase the things that he likes.
